--- Spirit Music ---
music by salvati & sbubby
icon by sal
programming by gabehaswon

This mod contains all of the music composed by Salvati and Sbubby for the Spirit Mod, now Spirit Classic and Spirit Reforged, that fits in base game.
Both of the aforementioned mods will continue having their own music for their biomes, events, and so on.

This mod also supports Music Display.

Check out Salvati and Sbubby! Fantastic musicians.
